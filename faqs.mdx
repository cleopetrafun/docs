    title="How to export my Cleo wallet?"
  >
You can export your wallet private key using settings side bar` --> `Export Private Key` button. From there you can import it to wallet providers like Phantom & Solflare.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="money-bill" title="What are the fees?">
    There is a 0.5% fee on opening LP positions. 

    There are no fees on claiming LP fees, closing and rebalancing a position.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="minus" title="How do I withdraw my funds?">
For now, export your in-app wallet to providers like Phantom.
  </Accordion>
    <Accordion icon="gas-pump" title="What is gas priority fee?">
A gas priority fee is paid to prioritize your transaction and result in fast execution of your Solana transaction.

You can choose from three available options (values in `SOL`)

Medium: `0.0001 (default)` || High: `0.0005` || Very High: `0.001`

  </Accordion>
